Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains the refund and cancellation rules for services provided by TrustEdge Infotech.

1. No Refund After Work Started

Once the service process has been started, submitted, processed or completed, the service fee will not be refundable.

2. Duplicate Payment

If a customer makes duplicate payment by mistake, the extra amount may be refunded after proper verification.

3. Failed Transaction

If payment is deducted from the customer account but not received by us, the customer should contact their bank or payment provider. If the amount is received by us, refund will be processed after verification.

4. Government Fees

Government charges, application fees, portal fees and third-party charges are non-refundable once paid or submitted.

5. Incorrect Details

If the customer provides wrong, incomplete or false information/documents, TrustEdge Infotech will not be responsible for rejection or delay, and refund may not be provided.

6. Cancellation

Cancellation request will be accepted only before the service work has started. Once work starts, cancellation will not be possible.

7. Refund Processing Time

If refund is approved, it may take 5 to 7 working days to reflect in the customer bank account or original payment method.
